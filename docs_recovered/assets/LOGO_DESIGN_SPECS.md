# CEREBRUM Logo Design Specifications

This document outlines five distinct brand identity concepts for the CEREBRUM reasoning framework.

---

## Idea 1: The "Glass-Box Flashlight" (Focus: Interpretability)
**Metaphor**: Illuminating truth in the dark.
**Description**: A dark slate-navy sphere of translucent nodes. A central glowing amber node radiates a cone of intense golden light, sharpening specific graph connections. Background nodes remain ghostly and gray, while connections inside the "flashlight beam" become hyper-detailed, pulsing gold.
**Vibe**: Clinical, precise, audit-ready.

## Idea 2: The "Structural Reflex" (Focus: Memory/REM Synthesis)
**Metaphor**: The brain creating a synaptic shortcut.
**Description**: Two distant, bright-blue community clusters separated by a dark void. A luminous golden arc (the "Shortcut") spontaneously forms between them, cutting through the void. The arc glows with a "synaptic pulse," showing energy moving from one community to the other.
**Vibe**: Biological, fast, learning, synthesizing.

## Idea 3: The "Tessellated Hive" (Focus: Community-Structure)
**Metaphor**: Perfect structural organization.
**Description**: A multi-layered geodesic sphere where each "face" or cluster is composed of tightly packed, perfectly organized nodes. The sphere isn't just lines; it’s a living structure that looks like a fusion of a crystal lattice and a beehive. One cluster is actively "pulsing" with amber energy, showing the active reasoning agent at work.
**Vibe**: Robust, architectural, efficient, scalable.

## Idea 4: The "Autonomous Prism" (Focus: GraphProfiler / Zero-Config)
**Metaphor**: Taking raw data and extracting intelligence.
**Description**: A beam of raw, unorganized gray nodes enters a triangular prism from the left. As the beam passes through the prism, it is refracted into highly ordered, organized, and glowing clusters of varying colors and intensity on the right. It represents the transformation from chaotic raw triples into autonomous, structured reasoning.
**Vibe**: Transformative, intelligent, automated, clean.

## Idea 5: The "Lattice Pulse" (Focus: Vectorized Performance)
**Metaphor**: Pure, high-speed kinetic intelligence.
**Description**: An abstract, isometric view of a high-speed matrix. The nodes are organized in a grid, but the edges connecting them are dynamic, glowing threads that appear to be in motion. The entire grid is slightly curved, hinting at the Fibonacci-sphere layout. It is dark-themed with neon cyan and gold accents, symbolizing the blend of structural data and neural-like speed.
**Vibe**: Modern, performance-focused, high-speed, futuristic.
