\begin{abstract}
We introduce CEREBRUM, a Community-Structured Graph Attention framework for Knowledge Graph (KG) reasoning. It implements a multi-hop traversal mechanism inspired by Transformer architectures, allowing KGs to perform complex reasoning without an LLM or training data. The engine fuses local and global topological signals to discover sub-graph communities, effectively acting as attention heads, and projects answers along deterministic verified paths.
\end{abstract}
